Explanation of Dataset Arrays

These numpy arrays describe the configurations used for generating the plots:

- IID: All experts share identical distributions, represented by [2, 2, 2, 5(remaining)].
- Non IID with Difference across significant players: Experts have varying distributions, here [1, 2, 3, 5 (remaining)], but consistent across experts.
- Non IID with Difference across significant players and experts: Different experts have distinct distributions, showcasing variations across both players and experts. [1, 2, 3, 5], [2.2, 2, 1.8, 5], [4, 1, 1, 5], [5.8, 0.1, 0.1, 5]
